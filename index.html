Incident Response Playbook Flowchart

Follow the steps below to respond to a compromised Microsoft 365 account.

Step 1
Immediately disable the user account and change the password.
Step 2
Gather information on the scope of the compromise, including the time of the compromise, affected systems, and potential data loss or exfiltration.
Step 3
Identify the root cause of the compromise and remediate the underlying vulnerability or misconfiguration.
Step 4
Collect and preserve evidence related to the compromise, including system logs and network traffic.
Step 5
Notify relevant stakeholders, including the affected user, IT team, and management, of the incident and its impact.
